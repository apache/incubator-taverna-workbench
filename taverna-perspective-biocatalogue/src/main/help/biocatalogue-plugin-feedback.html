BioCatalogue Plugin - Feedback

Please provide us with your feedback to help improve the BioCatalogue plugin. In order to do so, please go to About tab in the BioCatalogue perspective and click the "Leave feedback" button - you will be taken to a web page with a form to fill in and submit your comments.

Developers are very interested to hear:

  • suggestions regarding the existing functionality;
  • new feature requests;
  • ideas for improving the user interfaces;
  • any other feedback you may have.
